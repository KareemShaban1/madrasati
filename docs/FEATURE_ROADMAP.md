# Madrasati / EduCore Egypt — Feature Roadmap

Checklist of features to implement for a more professional and helpful platform.

---

## Core Learning Experience

- [ ] **Spaced repetition & review** — Remind students to revisit lessons or quiz questions at optimal intervals to improve long-term retention
- [ ] **Offline mode** — Allow downloading lessons and attachments for offline access on low connectivity
- [ ] **Progress analytics dashboard** — Show students time spent, lessons completed, quiz scores, and weak areas
- [ ] **Notes & highlighting** — Let students take notes and highlight text within lessons
- [ ] **Adaptive difficulty** — Adjust quiz difficulty based on performance and recommend easier/harder content

---

## Engagement & Motivation

- [ ] **Streaks & daily goals** — Daily login streaks and small daily goals to encourage regular use
- [ ] **Study plans** — Let students (or parents) set exam dates and get recommended study plans
- [ ] **Peer discussion / Q&A** — Discussion forums or Q&A per lesson or unit
- [ ] **Live tutoring / office hours** — Optional live sessions with teachers for extra help

---

## Content & Accessibility

- [ ] **Arabic & English UI** — Full bilingual support for the entire interface
- [ ] **Accessibility (a11y)** — Screen reader support, keyboard navigation, high-contrast mode, adjustable font sizes
- [ ] **Video features** — Playback speed, subtitles, chapter markers, and bookmarks for video lessons
- [ ] **Practice tests** — Full-length timed practice exams aligned to Egyptian curriculum and exams

---

## Parent & Teacher Tools

- [ ] **Parent dashboard** — Overview of progress, time spent, and performance for their children
- [ ] **Teacher tools** — Assign lessons, track class progress, and view analytics
- [ ] **Reporting** — Exportable reports (PDF) for parents and administrators

---

## Technical & UX

- [ ] **Mobile app (PWA)** — Progressive Web App for an installable experience on phones
- [ ] **Search** — Search across subjects, lessons, and content by keyword or topic
- [ ] **Bookmarks & favorites** — Save lessons or topics for quick access
- [ ] **Notifications** — Reminders for reviews, streaks, new content, or assignments

---

## Recommended Priority Order

1. Progress analytics dashboard
2. Search
3. Notes / bookmarks
4. Mobile-friendly PWA
5. Streaks & daily goals
6. Parent dashboard
